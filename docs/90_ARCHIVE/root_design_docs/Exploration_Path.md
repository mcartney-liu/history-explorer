# History Explorer - Exploration Path

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

---

# 1. Overview

History Explorer is designed around continuous exploration rather than traditional search.

Every historical topic should naturally lead to additional discoveries.

Users should never reach a dead end.

The exploration philosophy follows:

Curiosity

↓

Explore

↓

Connect

↓

Understand

↓

Discover

↓

New Curiosity

---

# 2. Purpose

The purpose of the Exploration Path is to transform historical learning into an ongoing exploration experience.

Instead of reading isolated information, users continuously discover connections across time, geography, civilizations, people, and events.

---

# 3. Exploration Philosophy

History should not be explored in a fixed order.

Every discovery becomes the starting point for another discovery.

Exploration is driven by curiosity.

Connections are more valuable than isolated facts.

Every historical entity can become a new exploration entry.

---

# 4. Exploration Principles

The platform follows these principles.

## Everything is a Node

Every entity represents an exploration opportunity.

## Every Node is Clickable

Every node can become the next exploration starting point.

## Exploration Never Ends

Users should always have meaningful next steps.

## Curiosity Drives Navigation

Users explore because they become curious.

## Connections Create Discovery

Unexpected relationships create valuable historical understanding.

# 6. Exploration Units

Users may continue exploration through different historical entities.

## Event

Historical events.

## Person

Historical figures.

## Civilization

Civilizations and societies.

## Location

Historical locations.

## Time Period

Historical eras and timelines.

Each unit becomes another exploration entry.

---

# 7. Product Goals

The exploration experience should:

- Increase exploration depth.
- Encourage unexpected discoveries.
- Reduce dead-end pages.
- Build continuous exploration.
- Help users understand historical relationships.

---

# 8. Future Expansion

Future versions may support:

- AI exploration recommendations.
- Personalized exploration paths.
- Knowledge Graph navigation.
- Visual exploration maps.
- Exploration history.
- Saved exploration paths.
